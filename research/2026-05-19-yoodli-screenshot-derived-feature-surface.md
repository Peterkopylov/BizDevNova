# Yoodli Feature Surface From Screenshots

Date: 2026-05-19
Company: Yoodli
Artifact type: Screenshot-derived feature teardown
Source basis: Inline screenshots from a Yoodli multi-persona roleplay session and analysis view

## Summary

The screenshots show that Yoodli is not just an AI roleplay tool. It combines multi-persona scenario practice, recorded session playback, transcript review, coaching, communication analytics, and in-line rewrite assistance. The visible product surface suggests a broader communication-coaching platform with roleplay as one entry point.

## Visible Features

### 1. Session and scenario layer

- Named scenario/session: `Big TV Debate`
- Custom scenario context: `Couple Shopping at Media Markt`
- Timestamped session record
- Share action for the session
- Left-navigation history / recordings surface

### 2. Roleplay playback and review

- Recorded roleplay video playback
- Timeline scrubber
- Play / pause controls
- Jump-back controls
- Speed control
- Volume control
- Fullscreen
- Comment-at-timestamp input
- Transcript view
- `Copy transcript`

### 3. Multi-persona / multi-party simulation

- Clear multi-persona retail scenario
- Separate participant turns in transcript
- Couple-shopping conversation, not just one customer
- Evidence that Yoodli supports multi-party roleplay in a practical commercial use case

### 4. Coaching layer

- `Coaching` tab with count of coaching items
- Strengths section
- Growth areas section
- Follow-up questions section
- Expandable feedback cards via `See more`
- `Copy feedback`

### 5. Quality-of-response coaching

- Product-detail coaching
- Recommendation-specific coaching
- Credibility / accuracy coaching
- Follow-up question preparation
- Guidance tied to concrete mistakes inside the session

Example visible themes:
- lead with concrete details
- know your product line
- build customer trust with specifics

### 6. Communication and speech coaching

- Pronunciation coaching
- Phoneme-specific guidance
- Audio playback for pronunciation help
- Tone analysis

This is important because it shows Yoodli is evaluating more than business logic. It also coaches delivery quality.

### 7. Tone analysis

Visible tone dimensions include:

- `Calm`
- `Helpful`
- `Under Pressure`
- `Confident`

This suggests a communication-style layer beyond raw transcript scoring.

### 8. Analytics layer

- Separate `Analytics` tab
- Subcategories:
  - `Word Choice`
  - `Listening`
  - `Delivery`
- Split between `What went well` and `What could have gone better`
- Weak words detection
- Conciseness measurement
- Repetition detection
- Filler-word detection
- Sentence-opener repetition detection
- Percentage-based metrics

Examples visible in screenshots:

- `5 weak words, 3%`
- `11% Excess`
- `8 repetition(s), 9%`
- `13 filler(s), 7%`
- sentence-opener repetition such as `"Yeah," 13%`

### 9. In-line rewrite assistance

Visible transcript-level assistant actions include:

- `Rephrase concisely`
- `Simplify to key points`
- `Rewrite as a sonnet`
- `Convert to riddle`

This suggests Yoodli includes an in-context language assistance layer, not just post-hoc scoring.

### 10. Iteration loop

- `Practise` / practice-again flow
- Session playback plus coaching plus analytics
- A clear feedback-to-retry workflow

This is commercially important because the product is built around repeated improvement, not just one-off evaluation.

## Product Interpretation

The screenshots suggest Yoodli is positioned at the intersection of:

- AI roleplay
- communication coaching
- speech analytics
- transcript intelligence
- iterative rehearsal

This is broader than a pure sales simulation product. The visible surface supports both business-scenario roleplay and general communication improvement.

## Strategic Takeaways

- Yoodli’s visible baseline is already much richer than simple AI roleplay.
- Multi-persona simulation is present in a practical scenario, not only as a marketing concept.
- The coaching and analytics surfaces are a major part of the product value.
- The product appears designed for repeated practice loops, which strengthens retention and manager value.
- Any competing product that only offers scenario chat without strong post-session analysis will likely feel thin by comparison.
