# GDevelop Competitor Note

## Summary

GDevelop is an `open-source, no-code and AI-assisted game engine` for creating and publishing 2D and 3D games across web, desktop, Steam, Android, and iOS.

Under the user-provided category, it is a strong direct fit for `Game creation tools`.

It matters because it is not selling just `AI generation` or `simple templates`. It is selling:

- a full game engine
- no-code game creation
- AI-assisted feature building
- publishing and monetization workflows
- cloud and collaboration services on top of an open-source core

## Pricing Signals

### Public Pricing

GDevelop publishes public pricing with free and paid plans:

- `Free`: free, open-source engine, Android/Desktop publishing once per day, multiplayer trial, free asset store access
- `Silver`: `200 AI credits per week`, `100 credits per month` for AI or asset store, `50` cloud games, Google Play / desktop / web publishing, unlimited multiplayer lobbies
- `Gold`: `1000 AI credits per week`, `300 credits per month`, `100` cloud games, iOS publishing, unlimited multiplayer lobbies and leaderboards, dedicated Discord channel, `1` free asset pack per month
- `Pro`: `3000 AI credits per week`, `1000 credits per month`, unlimited cloud projects, version history, unlimited multiplayer lobbies and leaderboards, dedicated pro support, collaboration

### Included Signals

Other verified pricing / commercial signals:

- save up to `40%` by paying yearly
- if a company has more than `$50K` of revenue or funds raised in the prior 12 months and uses a GDevelop account, `Pro` is required
- subscriptions can be canceled at any time
- professional support and custom game development are available commercially
