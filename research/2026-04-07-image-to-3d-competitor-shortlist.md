# Image-to-3D Competitor Shortlist

## Summary

This note collects services where the value is not just image generation, but turning:

- images into 3D assets
- sketches into 3D models
- text or images into production-ready 3D outputs

This is a distinct adjacent market worth tracking because monetization can come from:

- asset creation
- game-development workflows
- e-commerce 3D previews
- AR / VR content
- design and prototyping

## Shortlist

## 1. Tripo

What it appears to do:

- text-to-3D
- image-to-3D
- segmentation
- texturing
- production-ready 3D asset workflow

Why it is relevant:

- one of the clearest AI 3D workspace products
- strong workflow packaging around generation + editing + export

Source:

- [Tripo](https://www.tripo3d.ai/)

## 2. SphereLinks

What it appears to do:

- single-image to 3D model
- PBR textures
- export to Blender, Unity, Unreal, and web apps

Why it is relevant:

- strongly positioned around production-ready 3D from one image
- relevant for product design, game dev, e-commerce, architecture, and AR/VR

Source:

- [SphereLinks](https://www.spherelinks.io/)

## 3. Formy 3D

What it appears to do:

- text-to-3D
- image-to-3D
- all-in-one AI 3D model generation

Why it is relevant:

- broad accessible positioning for non-experts
- strong “professional 3D without 3D skills” angle

Source:

- [Formy 3D](https://formy3d.com/)

## 4. Triverse AI

What it appears to do:

- text-to-3D
- image-to-3D
- AI texturing
- PBR map generation

Why it is relevant:

- tries to cover more of the full 3D workflow, not only raw generation

Source:

- [Triverse AI](https://triverse.ai/)

## 5. PicToMesh

What it appears to do:

- image-to-3D model generation
- export to STL, GLB, USDZ, and OBJ

Why it is relevant:

- simple direct image-to-3D positioning
- useful as a lightweight pure-play conversion tool

Source:

- [PicToMesh](https://pictomesh.com/)

## 6. Modelfy

What it appears to do:

- image-to-3D conversion
- up to 300K polygon models
- asset management and multi-format export

Why it is relevant:

- more enterprise / precision-style messaging than many lightweight tools

Source:

- [Modelfy](https://modelfy.art/)

## 7. imgto3d.io

What it appears to do:

- free image-to-3D conversion
- browser preview
- multiple export formats

Why it is relevant:

- simple low-friction tool
- useful as a lightweight product benchmark

Source:

- [imgto3d.io](https://imgto3d.io/)

## 8. Meshcraft

What it appears to do:

- photo-to-3D
- PBR materials
- clean topology
- game-engine-ready exports
- API and batch processing

Why it is relevant:

- strong workflow packaging for game and pipeline use cases
- interesting because it connects generation to integration workflows

Source:

- [Meshcraft](https://meshcraft.xyz/)

## Related Platform Signal

Microsoft has also explored this direction through `Copilot 3D`, which suggests large platform players see 2D-to-3D as a meaningful adjacent category.

Source:

- [Windows Central on Copilot 3D](https://www.windowscentral.com/artificial-intelligence/microsoft-copilot/hands-on-with-copilot-3d-microsofts-ai-can-turn-a-single-photo-into-a-3d-model)

## Main Takeaway

This is a real adjacent product cluster, not just a niche feature.

The most important pattern is:

- these companies are not selling “AI art”
- they are selling usable 3D assets and workflow acceleration
