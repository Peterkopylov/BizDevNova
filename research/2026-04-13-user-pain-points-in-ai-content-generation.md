# User Pain Points in AI Content Generation

## Summary

This note captures a compact set of recurring user pain points in AI content generation.

These are useful as:

- messaging inputs
- onboarding language
- market-framing material
- product-prioritization signals

## Core Pain Points

- `High cost of top models`
- `Limited generations`
- `Not production-ready outputs`
- `Inconsistent results`
- `AI is confusing to me`
- `Other`

## Why This Matters

This cluster is useful because it covers three different layers of user pain:

- economic pain
- output-quality pain
- workflow/usability pain

In other words, users are not only frustrated by model quality itself. They are also frustrated by:

- affordability
- generation limits
- lack of business-ready output
- inconsistency
- product complexity

## Product Implication

Any product in this space can position itself against some combination of these pains:

- lower effective cost
- more usable volume
- more production-ready outputs
- stronger consistency
- simpler workflow

This makes the list useful as a compact market-pain framework, not just as onboarding copy.
