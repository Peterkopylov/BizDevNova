# Image Generator Services Shortlist

## Scope

This shortlist captures broad image-generator services not previously profiled in the research workspace.

## Newly Added Competitors

- [Midjourney](https://www.midjourney.com/)
- [Leonardo.Ai](https://leonardo.ai/)
- [Ideogram](https://ideogram.ai/)
- [Krea](https://www.krea.ai/)
- [Recraft](https://www.recraft.ai/)
- [Freepik AI](https://www.freepik.com/ai/image-generator)
- [Playground](https://playground.com/)
- [NightCafe](https://creator.nightcafe.studio/)

## Why These Matter

- `Midjourney`: benchmark for image quality and creator mindshare
- `Leonardo.Ai`: strong control/training/workflow competitor
- `Ideogram`: typography and design-output wedge
- `Krea`: broad creative suite with realtime and multi-model access
- `Recraft`: AI for professional designers, vectors, and brand consistency
- `Freepik AI`: suite-level competitor with licensing and scale
- `Playground`: lightweight mainstream creation competitor
- `NightCafe`: community-first AI image platform
