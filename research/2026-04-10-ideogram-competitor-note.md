# Ideogram Competitor Note

## Summary

Ideogram is a text-to-image platform best known for strong typography inside generated images, logo/poster use cases, and growing consistency features like character reference.

It matters because it sells:

- text-accurate image generation
- design and branding-friendly output
- accessible image generation with strong practical use cases

## Pricing Signals

### Public Pricing

Ideogram publishes clear official pricing:

- `Free`: free, `10` slow credits/week
- `Basic`: `$8/month` or `$84/year`, `400` priority credits/month, `100` slow credits/day
- `Plus`: `$20/month` or `$180/year`, `1,000` priority credits/month, unlimited slow credits
- `Pro`: `$60/month` or `$504/year`, `3,500` priority credits/month, unlimited slow credits
- `Team`: `$30/month/member` with minimum `2` seats, or `$240/year/member`, `1,500` priority credits/month/member
- top-ups range from `$4 / 100 credits` to `$4 / 250 credits` depending on plan

### Included Signals

- unlimited slow generations start at `Plus`
- usage rights are not restricted across plans
- concurrency scales heavily by plan
