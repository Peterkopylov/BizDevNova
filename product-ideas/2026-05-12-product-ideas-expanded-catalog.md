# Product Ideas Expanded Catalog

## Summary

This note captures an expanded working catalog of product and business ideas from the current Nova / Delightex exploration.

Some of these overlap with earlier notes in the folder, but they are kept here as a single comprehensive reference list so the project folder has one place where the broader idea inventory is visible.

## Platform and Business Directions

### Sales & Stakeholder Visualization

Turn presentations or business input into explorable 3D environments for sales, stakeholder communication, and decision support.

Short framing:

- `presentation -> 3D`
- business storytelling through space rather than flat slides

### Visual AI Workspace

Create a `virtual office` where AI agents, tasks, artifacts, and workflows are represented visually.

Short framing:

- multi-agent workspace
- persistent visual workflow layer

### Visualization Generator (Static)

Generate controlled static visualizations from structured scenes or business inputs.

### 3D Interface Builder

Tool for creating user interfaces directly in 3D space, using the existing 3D UI framework as a wedge.

### Visual Configurator Builder for SMB

Builder for small and medium businesses that need visual configurators without enterprise complexity.

### 3D Dashboards / Spatial Analytics

Dashboards where business or operational information is represented spatially rather than in flat BI views.

### 3D Miro Board

Collaborative visual workspace in 3D for arranging ideas, media, spaces, and relationships.

This overlaps with:

- `3d "Miro board"`
- `3d canvas`

### Selling SDK

SDK or embedded layer that helps other products or partners sell through interactive 3D or guided visual experiences.

### Nova - Young Creator

A more focused Nova direction aimed at younger creators.

### Training Creation Platform

Platform for building interactive trainings, simulations, and guided educational experiences.

### Museum / Visitor Experience

Tools or packaged experiences for museums, exhibitions, and public visitor engagement.

### Shopify / Webflow-like Product for Publishing Trainings and Simulations

Publishing platform for people who want to launch and distribute trainings, simulations, and interactive learning experiences.

### Smart Snapshot NSFW

A specialized or adult-content-related variation around Smart Snapshot.

This should be treated as a separate strategic topic because the distribution, moderation, and policy constraints are very different.

## Education and Learning Ideas

### Immersive Language Learning

Language learning through situated environments such as:

- cafe
- airport
- store
- public space

### Code-Learning App with AI

Learning product for coding, likely with interactive scenarios or guided creation instead of static exercises.

### Interactive Model Builder for Training Purposes

Training-oriented builder for interactive models, with the firefighter case as one concrete use case.

### AI-Generated Explanatory Courses

Generate full courses or learning experiences automatically.

Example pattern:

- German course as a sequence of NPC dialogues

## Workflow-Led Product Directions

### Workflow: 3D to 2D

Turn structured 3D scenes into controllable 2D outputs.

### Workflow: 3D to Video

Turn structured 3D scenes into controllable video outputs.

### Workflow: Text to Game

Generate playable games from text, ideally with editable downstream logic and world structure.

### Workflow: Text / Image to 3D Scene

Turn text or image input into an editable 3D scene.

### Workflow: Text to Simulation

Generate scenario-based simulations from text input.

### Workflow: Presentation to 3D Experience

Turn decks, documents, or business content into interactive 3D storytelling.

### Workflow: 3D Website Builder

Generate or assemble interactive 3D websites from content and structure.

### Workflow: 2D to Interactive Model

Turn flat 2D input into something interactive and model-like, not just visual.
