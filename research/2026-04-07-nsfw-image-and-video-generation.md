# NSFW Image and Video Generation

## Summary

`NSFW` usually means `Not Safe For Work`.

In the context of image and video generation, it typically refers to AI-generated content that is considered inappropriate for a normal workplace setting.

## What It Usually Includes

In generative image and video products, `NSFW` most often refers to:

- erotic content
- pornographic content
- nudity
- sexualized scenes
- fetish or adult-oriented material

## Why It Matters in Product Research

NSFW generation is important to track because it often creates a separate product category with different:

- moderation rules
- platform restrictions
- payment risks
- legal/compliance issues
- brand positioning choices

## Strategic Relevance

Many generative products make an explicit choice here:

- allow NSFW generation
- restrict it
- ban it completely

That choice can affect:

- target market
- distribution channels
- trust and safety messaging
- enterprise readiness

## Main Takeaway

`NSFW image/video generation` is not just a content label.

It is also a product and market segmentation factor in generative AI.
