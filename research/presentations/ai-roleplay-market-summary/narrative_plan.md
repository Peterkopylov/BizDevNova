# AI Roleplay Market Summary

Audience:

- internal product and strategy team

Objective:

- summarize the market we studied
- highlight key competitors and funding posture
- clarify the core value propositions and table-stakes features
- recommend where we should play first and where we can differentiate

Narrative arc:

1. The category is real and growing, but fragmented
2. Competitors cluster around training and readiness, not generic AI chat
3. The strongest commercial wedge today is sales and customer-facing readiness
4. Table stakes are now clear: scenario, persona, simulation, scoring, dashboard
5. Our best place is a focused first wedge plus a sharper long-term differentiator

Slide list:

1. Title: AI Roleplay Market Summary
2. Market overview: segments, strongest demand, strategic shift
3. Competitor map: who matters and what they focus on
4. Funding and market maturity: incumbents to startups
5. What customers actually buy: core value propositions
6. Table-stakes features to enter credibly
7. Our place: best first wedge and long-term differentiator

- cite source groupings in speaker notes

- warm paper background
- dark ink typography
- green accent for confidence / readiness
- gold and coral for secondary emphasis
- clean editorial cards with subtle structure
- minimal decorative visuals; prioritize clarity and editability
