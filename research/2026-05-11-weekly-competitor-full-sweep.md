## Biggest Changes This Week

### OpenArt

- The official [`What's New`](https://openart.ai/whats-new) page added `UNLIMITED CREATIONS + 50% OFF WONDER ANNUAL. Ends May 15th` on `2026-05-06`.
- OpenArt is explicitly offering unlimited generations on `Nano Banana Pro`, `Seedance 2.0`, `GPT Image 2`, and more for `Wonder` and `Infinite` plans.
- This is a real packaging and conversion signal, not just feature copy. OpenArt is using temporary unlimited access to frontier partner models to push higher-tier plan adoption.

### Fotor

- The live [`Fotor pricing`](https://www.fotor.com/pricing/) page now foregrounds `AI Agent Sisi` as a plan-level usage dimension, with chat and task allowances across tiers.
- The same page now leans much harder into `Your Favorite Models, All in One Place`, listing current image and video models such as `GPT Image 2`, `Nano Banana Pro`, `Seedream 5.0 Lite`, `Veo 3.1`, and `Seedance 2.0`.
- This sharpens Fotor's positioning from `broad lightweight editor with AI` toward `low-friction model aggregator plus editing suite`.
