# Leonardo.Ai Competitor Note

## Summary

Leonardo.Ai is a broad generative media platform for image generation, editing, custom model training, and increasingly video and motion workflows.

It matters because it sells more than raw generation. It sells:

- exploration with control
- custom training
- private generation
- model variety under one roof

## Why It Matters

Leonardo is one of the stronger image-generation competitors for our space because it combines:

- image generation
- training

- workflow depth
- commercial positioning

It sits closer to `control + consistency + workflow` than pure prompt-only tools.

## Pricing Signals

### Public Pricing

Leonardo publishes detailed official pricing:

- `Free`: `$0`, `150` fast tokens/day
- `Essential`: `$12/month`, `8,500` fast tokens/month, `10` personal AI models
- `Premium`: `$30/month`, `25,000` fast tokens/month, `20` personal AI models, unlimited relaxed image generation on select models
- `Ultimate`: `$60/month`, `60,000` fast tokens/month, `50` personal AI models, unlimited relaxed image and video generation on select models
- `Starter Team`: `$24/seat/month`, billed as `$72/month`, `75,000` shared tokens
- `Growth Team`: `$48/seat/month`, billed as `$144/month`, `180,000` shared tokens
- `API Pay as you go`: starts with `$5` API credit, usage-based

### Included Signals

- private generations start on paid individual plans
- custom model training is heavily gated by tier
- relaxed unlimited generation is an important premium wedge

## Rights / Commercial Use

Leonardo is explicit about ownership differences by plan:

- paid subscribers retain full ownership and IP rights in generated images
- free users get commercial-use access, but Leonardo retains broader rights over those free-tier creations

Source:

- [Leonardo pricing](https://leonardo.ai/pricing)

## Control / Consistency Relevance

Leonardo is highly relevant to the thesis because it emphasizes:

## GTM Insight

Leonardo's GTM appears to combine:

1. self-serve creator adoption

## Implications for Us

- Leonardo is one of the more important image-gen competitors for our thesis
- it validates that control, private generation, and model training are monetizable wedges
- it also shows that high review trust can coexist with a broad consumer/creator product

## Valuation / Funding Signal

Leonardo.Ai was acquired by Canva in July 2024.

Official acquisition terms were not disclosed. Public reporting said Leonardo had raised about `~$38.8M` before the deal, and later commentary suggested the acquisition was thought to be worth around `~$370M`, but that figure was not officially confirmed.
