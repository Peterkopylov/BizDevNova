# Wave AI Competitor Note

## Summary

Wave AI is a lightweight AI photo and image editing product focused on:

- product photo enhancement
- background replacement
- object removal
- style transformation
- text-to-image

It is relevant as an adjacent competitor in the lightweight marketing and product-visuals workflow category.

Sources:

- [Wave AI homepage](https://www.waveai.io/)

## Why It Matters

Wave AI matters because it represents the low-friction end of AI image tooling:

- upload a photo
- change the background
- remove an object
- create a more ad-like visual

It is not positioned as a deep control-layer product or an enterprise workflow system.

## Key Selling Point

The clearest selling point is:

- `easy AI photo editing and product-photo-to-ad transformation`

A second strong wedge is:

- `improve simple images quickly without design skills`

## ICP

The most likely ideal customer profiles are:

- small merchants
- casual creators
- social media users
- users making simple product ads
- users wanting fast photo editing and restyling

This appears more consumer and prosumer than enterprise.

## Implications for Us
