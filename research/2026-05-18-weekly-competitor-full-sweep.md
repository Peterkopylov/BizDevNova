# Weekly Competitor Full Sweep

## Summary

Materially new changes versus the latest local known state from the `2026-05-15` daily scan and the `2026-05-11` weekly sweep were found in `FLORA` and `Runway`.

- `FLORA` published a May 5 pricing reset that moves the product off credits and into seat-priced plans with shared dollar-denominated usage pools, temporary included-usage boosts, and time-bounded `Nano Banana` promo packaging.
- `Runway` published a May 14 Japan expansion announcement with a Tokyo headquarters, an initial `$40M` local investment, and explicit enterprise-growth claims for the region.

## Biggest Changes This Week

### FLORA

- The official [`FLORA pricing`](https://flora.ai/pricing) page and [`A new pricing model for FLORA, plus Nano Banana on us`](https://flora.ai/blog/a-new-pricing-model-for-flora-plus-nano-banana-on-us) post now show a materially different monetization model from the older local note.
- FLORA is no longer primarily packaging itself around credits. It now sells `Starter` / `Pro` / `Max` as seat-priced plans with a shared dollar-denominated usage pool, plus temporary extra included usage through `2026-07-01`.
- The higher tiers also use `Nano Banana 2` / `Nano Banana Pro` promo access as a conversion lever, which is a meaningful GTM move rather than a cosmetic pricing-page rewrite.

### Runway

- The official [`Runway is Coming to Japan`](https://runwayml.com/news/runway-is-coming-to-japan) post on `2026-05-14` is a real market-structure and GTM signal, not just a generic regional announcement.
- Runway says Japan is now its third-largest market, the fastest-growing self-serve market in Asia, and that enterprise customers there grew `300%` in the last 12 months.
- The company is opening a Tokyo office as its Japan headquarters and committing an initial `$40M` to build its local presence, which makes this a stronger signal than ordinary hiring or partner copy.
