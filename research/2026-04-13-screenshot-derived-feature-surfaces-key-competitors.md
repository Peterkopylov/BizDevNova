## Cross-Competitor Pattern

These screenshots reinforce a strong pattern:

- the most important competitors are no longer selling one generator
- they are selling `bundled capability surfaces`

The strongest recurring UI buckets are:

- generation
- edit / enhancement
- character
- video
- finetuning or model customization
- asset or workflow management

## Product Implication

The market is converging toward:

- multimodal creative suites
- structured tool collections
- reusable workflows
- more explicit capability packaging

That supports the broader thesis that value is moving upward from:

- pure generation

toward:

- creative operating layers
- workflow systems
- task-specific production surfaces
