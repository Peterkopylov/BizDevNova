# Midjourney Competitor Note

## Summary

Midjourney is one of the best-known AI image generation platforms, centered on high-quality text-to-image creation with a strong creative community and increasingly broader web tooling.

It matters because it does not primarily sell workflow software. It sells:

- premium image quality
- strong aesthetic output
- a creator-centric generation experience
- community-led discovery and sharing

Sources:

- [Midjourney plans](https://docs.midjourney.com/docs/plans)
- [Midjourney terms](https://docs.midjourney.com/docs/terms-of-service)
- [Using images and videos commercially](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/en-us/articles/27870375276557-Using-Images-Videos-Commercially)
- [Website overview](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/en-us/articles/33329460426765-Website-Overview)

## Why It Matters

Midjourney is a core benchmark for:

- image quality
- creator mindshare
- artistic and stylized output

It is less relevant as a deep workflow or control-layer platform, but highly relevant as a category-defining image generator.

## Pricing Signals

### Public Pricing

Midjourney publishes clear official pricing:

- `Basic`: `$10/month` or `$96/year`, `3.3 hr/month` fast GPU time
- `Standard`: `$30/month` or `$288/year`, `15 hr/month` fast GPU time, unlimited image relax mode
- `Pro`: `$60/month` or `$576/year`, `30 hr/month` fast GPU time, stealth mode, unlimited image and SD video relax mode
- `Mega`: `$120/month` or `$1,152/year`, `60 hr/month` fast GPU time, stealth mode, unlimited image and SD video relax mode
- extra fast GPU time: `$4/hour`

### Included Signals

- Stealth Mode is limited to `Pro` and `Mega`
- unlimited relaxed image generation starts at `Standard`
- companies over `$1M` in revenue need `Pro` or `Mega` for commercial company usage

## Rights / Commercial Use

Midjourney states that users own the assets they create, subject to its terms.

Important caveat:

- companies over `$1M` in gross annual revenue must be on `Pro` or `Mega` to own/use assets commercially for the company

Sources:

- [Terms of service](https://docs.midjourney.com/docs/terms-of-service)
- [Using images and videos commercially](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/en-us/articles/27870375276557-Using-Images-Videos-Commercially)

## Control / Consistency Relevance

Midjourney is relevant as a quality benchmark, but weaker than some newer players on:

- explicit structure control
- workflow packaging
- enterprise process integration

It is stronger on:

- aesthetic quality
- creator appeal
- broad prompt-based generation

## GTM Insight

Midjourney's GTM remains strongly:

1. creator-led
2. subscription-based
3. community-driven
4. quality/reputation-driven

## Implications for Us

- Midjourney is a must-track benchmark for image quality and creator mindshare
- it is less dangerous than workflow platforms in areas like team packaging or structured generation
- it reinforces that pure-generation leaders can still have weak review sentiment around billing, support, and usability
