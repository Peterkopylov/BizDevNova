# G2 Virtual Tour Category: Potential Competitors

## Summary

This note captures a list of potential competitors and adjacent players discovered through G2's `Virtual Tour` and related alternatives pages.

These should be treated as:

- potential competitors
- adjacent players
- discovery leads

not as already-validated direct competitors.

## Why This Matters

The `virtual tour` category touches several adjacent markets relevant to the broader thesis:

- real estate visualization
- interactive space exploration
- digital twins
- guided sales environments
- immersive presentation workflows

This category is especially relevant to:

- `interactive / simulation / configurator`

and partly relevant to:

- image / visualization as a sales tool

This repetition makes them stronger shortlist candidates for future profiling.

## Why These Are Interesting

### Matterport

- strongest repeated name
- well-known digital-twin / space-capture platform
- likely one of the most important adjacent players in immersive visualization

### Concept3D

- appears repeatedly in virtual-tour alternatives
- likely relevant for campus, venue, and real-estate-like guided digital experiences

### Shapespark

- likely very relevant for browser-based interactive 3D / architectural walkthroughs
- useful bridge between static visualization and interactive space exploration

### CloudPano / TeliportMe / Kuula / iStaging

- likely important in lower-friction virtual tour publishing and sharing workflows
- useful to understand the more productized and lighter-weight end of the market

## Important Note

This note is intentionally a discovery note, not a validation note.

The presence of a company on G2 alternatives pages does not automatically mean:

- direct strategic overlap
- same buyer
- same product category

Each company should still be profiled independently before being treated as a serious competitor.
