# Multiplayer Experience for Website Visitors

## Summary

Product idea: turn a website from a single-user browsing experience into a lightweight multiplayer environment where visitors can be present together, interact, and explore in shared space.

## Core Concept

Instead of treating a website as a static or solo interface, the product would add a real-time shared layer:

- visitors can see other visitors
- users can interact in a shared environment
- browsing becomes a social or collaborative experience

This could make a website feel more like a live place than a set of pages.

## Why It Could Matter

- makes websites more memorable and differentiated
- increases engagement through social presence
- creates new forms of product discovery, support, and community interaction
- can make branded experiences feel more immersive

## Potential Use Cases

- product demos with multiple visitors present at the same time
- virtual showrooms or brand spaces
- event microsites with live attendee interaction
- collaborative onboarding or guided tours
- community spaces layered on top of content
- ecommerce experiences where users browse together

## Product Angles

### Social Layer for Existing Websites

Add a multiplayer presence layer to ordinary websites without requiring a full rebuild.

### 3D Shared Website Experience

Turn websites or landing pages into explorable shared spaces.

### Guided Multiplayer Commerce or Sales

Use multiplayer presence for sales assistance, product tours, and conversion support.

## MVP

- website visitors can join the same shared space
- presence indicators show who else is there
- simple movement, cursor, or avatar system
- lightweight chat, reactions, or guided interaction
- embeddable setup for existing sites

## Risks

- may feel gimmicky if the social layer does not add clear value
- many websites do not naturally benefit from synchronous presence
- performance and moderation could become important quickly
- onboarding friction must stay very low

## Open Questions

- Is the first wedge ecommerce, events, education, SaaS onboarding, or brand experiences?
- Should the first version be 2D presence, cursor-based collaboration, or full 3D?
- Is the main value social discovery, conversion lift, support, or entertainment?
