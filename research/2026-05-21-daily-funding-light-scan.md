## Material Competitor Changes

- `Runway`: the important new product/positioning signal is `Runway Agent`, launched on `2026-05-13`. Runway is now packaging itself not just as a model/API layer, but as an agentic creative partner that can propose a concept, develop story beats, set visual direction, and assemble a finished video from one conversation. This is a meaningful wedge expansion beyond `Characters`, `Builders`, and `Fund` ([source](https://runwayml.com/news/introducing-runway-agent)).
- `Canva AI`: the strongest newly surfaced GTM/distribution signal is the `Canva and Claude for Small Business` launch on `2026-05-13`. Canva is explicitly extending the `Canva Design Engine` into SMB campaign creation inside Anthropic's distribution surface, which deepens the thesis that Canva wants to be present wherever business users plan and make content, not only inside Canva-owned workflows ([source](https://www.canva.com/newsroom/news/)).
- `Higgsfield`: the strongest newly surfaced workflow signal is the `Claude MCP` connector post from `2026-05-08`, which frames Claude as a front-end to Higgsfield's full production environment with `30+` video and image models, up to `4K` output, and no API-key setup burden for the end user. Combined with Higgsfield's public `MCP & CLI` navigation, this is a material agent-distribution and orchestration move rather than a cosmetic integration note ([sources](https://higgsfield.ai/blog/Generate-AI-Videos-From-Claude-with-Higgsfield-MCP), [homepage](https://higgsfield.ai/)).
- `Twikit`: this pass confirmed a material `multi-surface accumulated shift` that the prior daily scan did not elevate. Twikit now clearly packages the platform as `TwikBot Create`, `TwikBot Hub`, and `TwikBot Live`, with partner-led build, customer-team-managed back-office operations, and customer/dealer-facing live configuration as separate but connected layers. That is a meaningful packaging and GTM clarification for the configurator-builder thread, not just a copy refresh ([sources](https://twikit.com/twikbot-hub/), [use cases](https://twikit.com/use-cases/), [about](https://twikit.com/about)).
