# Open Research Questions Batch

## Summary

This note collects research questions that should be explored further before turning them into product or strategy conclusions.

## Questions

### Why Did BrainPOP Come to Us?

Research question about the real reasons BrainPOP chose to engage with us.

Possible angles:

- what problem were they trying to solve?
- which part of our product or positioning was most attractive?
- was the driver speed, simplicity, cost, education fit, or something else?

### Why Did Godot Emerge Despite Unity Already Existing?

Research question about why a meaningful alternative like Godot appeared and gained traction despite Unity's prior market position.

Possible angles:

- developer pain with Unity
- openness and trust
- pricing and governance
- simplicity and accessibility
- community and ecosystem dynamics

### How Do Editors Look in Different Contexts?

Research question about how editors are designed across different categories and environments.

Possible angles:

- game editors
- design editors
- education-oriented editors
- mobile-first editors
- consumer creative tools
- enterprise workflow tools

Goal:

- identify recurring patterns
- understand what makes an editor feel approachable or powerful
- find useful references for our own editor direction
